THE MANIFESTO

Re-engineering the fundamental substrate of human coordination for the 22nd century using modular consensus architectures.

01 // Asynchronous Finality

KarChain introduces a paradigm shift with Hyper-Graph Sharding. Unlike linear blockchains, our state is partitioned into thousands of concurrent execution threads.

Using Proof-of-Synchrony, we achieve mathematical finality in sub-second timeframes, eliminating the need for probabilistic confirmations found in Nakamoto Consensus.

02 // Post-Quantum Security

The era of elliptic curve cryptography is ending. KarChain is built as a Quantum Fortress, utilizing Lattice-based signatures (Kyber/Dilithium) to secure assets against future quantum decryption threats.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to add KarChain to MetaMask?
The easiest way is to click "Connect Wallet" on our dashboard. The site will automatically prompt MetaMask to add the KarChain Testnet (Chain ID: 1515). Alternatively, you can add it manually: RPC URL: http://127.0.0.1:8545
How to get Testnet Tokens (KAR)?
Once you connect your wallet and complete the "Social Identity" task on the dashboard, our automated faucet will drop initial KAR tokens to your address for gas fees.
What are the validator requirements?
Running a Besu node requires 4GB RAM, 2 CPU Cores, and 50GB SSD. Docker is recommended for easy setup.
